Ads Audit runs a structured, parallel analysis across all your active advertising platforms, evaluating account structure, campaign settings, targeting configurations, bidding strategies, conversion tracking, and creative health. Unlike the broader Ads skill, Ads Audit follows a systematic checklist methodology that ensures no configuration detail is overlooked, producing a scored assessment with pass/fail criteria for each dimension.

Use Ads Audit when taking over an existing ad account, during annual account health reviews, or when performance has degraded and you need to identify the root cause systematically. It is particularly valuable for agencies onboarding new clients or internal teams inheriting accounts from previous managers.

Example: Run Ads Audit on a newly inherited Google Ads account to receive a scored report revealing that enhanced conversions are not configured (losing 20-30% of conversion signal), 40% of ad groups contain only one ad variation (preventing proper A/B testing), negative keyword lists have not been updated in 6 months, and audience exclusions are missing causing ads to show to existing customers.
