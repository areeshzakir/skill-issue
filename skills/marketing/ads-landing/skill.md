Ads Landing evaluates the landing pages your paid campaigns direct traffic to, assessing message match with ad copy, page load speed, mobile responsiveness, conversion rate optimization elements, trust signals, form friction, and above-the-fold clarity. It identifies disconnects between what the ad promises and what the landing page delivers, which is the most common source of wasted ad spend.

Use Ads Landing when your click-through rates are healthy but conversion rates are below benchmarks, when launching new campaign landing pages, or when Google Ads reports low landing page experience quality scores. It bridges the gap between media buying and conversion optimization.

Example: Run Ads Landing against your top 10 campaign landing pages to find that 3 pages take over 4 seconds to load on mobile (increasing bounce rate by 53%), your search ad promises "Free 14-day trial" but the landing page headline says "Get Started" with no mention of the trial, the demo request form asks for 8 fields when industry benchmarks show 3-4 fields maximize completion, and 2 pages lack social proof elements entirely.
