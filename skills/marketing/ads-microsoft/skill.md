Ads Microsoft analyzes your Microsoft Advertising campaigns across Bing Search, Performance Max, Audience Network, and Copilot placements. It evaluates the effectiveness of Google Ads imports (a common setup), identifies Microsoft-specific optimization opportunities, assesses audience targeting using LinkedIn profile data (a unique Microsoft advantage), and reviews emerging Copilot ad placement performance.

Use Ads Microsoft when you have imported Google Ads campaigns but have not optimized them specifically for the Microsoft ecosystem, when you want to leverage Microsoft's unique LinkedIn audience targeting capabilities, or when exploring Copilot as a new ad placement channel. Microsoft often offers lower CPCs than Google for comparable audiences, making optimization high-value.

Example: Run Ads Microsoft to discover that your imported Google campaigns include 15 ad groups with keywords that have zero search volume on Bing (wasting management overhead), you are not utilizing LinkedIn profile targeting to layer job function and industry on search campaigns (a Microsoft-exclusive feature), your bids are set at Google levels which are 30% higher than necessary for the same positions on Bing, and Copilot placements are available for your vertical but not yet activated.
