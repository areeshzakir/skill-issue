Ads performs a comprehensive, cross-platform audit of your entire paid advertising operation. It analyzes campaigns across Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn, TikTok, Microsoft Ads, and YouTube simultaneously, identifying inefficiencies in spend allocation, audience targeting, creative performance, and conversion tracking. The output is a unified report that highlights cross-platform opportunities and conflicts.

Use Ads when you need a holistic view of your paid media performance rather than a platform-specific deep dive. It is ideal for quarterly business reviews, when onboarding a new marketing lead, or when you suspect budget is being wasted due to audience overlap or attribution conflicts between platforms.

Example: Run Ads against your Q1 campaign data to discover that your Google Search and Meta retargeting campaigns are competing for the same bottom-funnel users (inflating CPAs by 35%), your LinkedIn spend is concentrated on a single audience segment while ignoring a high-intent lookalike, and your TikTok creative refresh rate is too slow causing frequency fatigue after day 7.
