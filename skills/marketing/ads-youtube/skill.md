Ads YouTube analyzes your YouTube advertising campaigns across all formats including skippable in-stream, non-skippable bumpers, in-feed discovery, Shorts ads, and connected TV placements. It evaluates video creative effectiveness (hook rate, view-through rate, click-through rate), audience targeting precision, frequency management across Google Video Partners, and the integration between YouTube campaigns and your broader Google Ads conversion strategy.

Use Ads YouTube when video is a key part of your media strategy, when brand awareness and consideration metrics matter alongside direct response, when you are producing video content but struggling to get efficient distribution, or when evaluating whether YouTube Shorts ads can complement your TikTok strategy.

Example: Run Ads YouTube to discover that your 30-second in-stream ads lose 60% of viewers in the first 5 seconds (recommendation: restructure the hook), your frequency cap is set to 3 per week but Google Video Partners are delivering additional uncapped impressions, your bumper ads are driving strong brand lift but not being credited in last-click attribution (recommendation: add Brand Lift study), and connected TV placements deliver 40% higher view-through rates at comparable CPMs.
