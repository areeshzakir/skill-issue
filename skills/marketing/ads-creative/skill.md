Ads Creative evaluates the quality, diversity, and freshness of your advertising creative assets across all platforms. It analyzes ad copy effectiveness, image and video quality, creative-to-audience alignment, format utilization, A/B testing coverage, creative fatigue indicators, and adherence to platform-specific best practices. The output includes specific rewrite suggestions and new creative concepts.

Use Ads Creative when click-through rates are declining (a common sign of creative fatigue), when launching campaigns for a new product or audience segment, or during monthly creative refresh cycles. It ensures your creative portfolio is diverse enough to reach different audience mindsets and fresh enough to maintain engagement.

Example: Run Ads Creative on your Meta Ads account to find that 70% of your ad sets use the same three images (causing frequency fatigue above 4.0), your headline copy focuses exclusively on features rather than benefits, you are not utilizing carousel or collection formats which outperform single-image ads in your vertical by 2.3x, and your video ads lack captions despite 85% of mobile users watching without sound.
