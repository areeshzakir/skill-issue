Ads Budget analyzes your current budget distribution and bidding strategies across all advertising platforms, identifying misallocations, underspending on high-performing campaigns, overspending on diminishing returns, and suboptimal bid strategy selections. It models alternative allocation scenarios and projects their impact on key metrics like CPA, ROAS, and total conversions.

Use Ads Budget during monthly or quarterly budget planning cycles, when total ad spend is increasing and you need to decide where to allocate incremental dollars, or when leadership requests a justification for current spend distribution. It is essential when scaling budgets up or down to understand which levers to pull first.

Example: Run Ads Budget with your current $50K monthly spend across Google ($25K), Meta ($15K), and LinkedIn ($10K) to discover that your Google brand campaigns are over-budgeted by $5K (hitting impression share ceiling at $20K), that $3K reallocated from LinkedIn awareness to Meta retargeting would yield 40 additional conversions, and that switching from manual CPC to target CPA on your top Google campaigns could reduce cost per acquisition by 18%.
