NPS Analysis processes your Net Promoter Score survey responses with a focus on detractor sentiment, performing categorized theme extraction, trend identification, and actionable insight generation. It goes beyond simple score tracking to understand why detractors are unhappy, grouping complaints into product, service, pricing, and experience themes, and tracking how these themes shift week over week.

Use NPS Analysis on a weekly cadence to stay ahead of emerging customer satisfaction issues before they become churn drivers. It is especially valuable when your NPS score drops unexpectedly, when preparing customer experience reports for leadership, or when product teams need prioritized customer pain points to inform the roadmap.

Example: Run NPS Analysis on this week's 150 survey responses to discover that detractor volume increased 18% driven primarily by a new "slow load times" theme that appeared after last Tuesday's release (12 mentions, up from 0), the persistent "billing confusion" theme now accounts for 25% of all detractor comments (recommendation: prioritize invoice redesign), and promoter comments increasingly mention the new onboarding flow as a differentiator worth amplifying in marketing.
