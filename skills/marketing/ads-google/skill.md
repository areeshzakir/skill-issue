Ads Google performs a deep-dive analysis of your Google Ads account across all campaign types including Search, Performance Max, Display, YouTube, and Shopping. It evaluates account structure, keyword strategy, quality scores, ad rank, auction insights, asset performance in PMax, audience signals, conversion tracking configuration, and search term reports to identify wasted spend and growth opportunities.

Use Ads Google for a thorough platform-specific audit when Google is your primary acquisition channel, when migrating from legacy campaign types to Performance Max, when quality scores are declining, or when you need to diagnose why CPA has increased despite stable budgets.

Example: Run Ads Google to discover that your Performance Max campaigns are cannibalizing branded search traffic (visible in auction insights), 23% of your search budget is going to irrelevant queries that should be added as negative keywords, your responsive search ads have "poor" asset performance on 4 of 15 headlines, and your Display campaigns lack frequency capping causing the same users to see your ads 12+ times per week.
