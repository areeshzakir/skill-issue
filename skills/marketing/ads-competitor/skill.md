Ads Competitor gathers and analyzes competitive advertising intelligence across search, social, and display channels. It examines competitor ad copy patterns, landing page strategies, estimated spend levels, audience targeting approaches, creative formats, promotional cadence, and market positioning. The analysis reveals gaps in your competitive strategy and identifies opportunities to differentiate.

Use Ads Competitor when entering a new market, when a competitor's aggressive ad spend is impacting your performance, during annual strategy planning, or when you need to benchmark your creative and messaging approach against the competitive set. It helps answer the question of where competitors are investing and what you can learn from their approach.

Example: Run Ads Competitor targeting your top 5 SaaS competitors to discover that three of them are bidding aggressively on your brand terms (with specific ad copy examples), competitor A is running video-first creative on Meta while you rely on static images, competitor B's landing pages emphasize a free trial CTA while yours pushes demo requests, and none of your competitors are active on TikTok representing an untapped channel opportunity.
