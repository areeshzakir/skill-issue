Ads LinkedIn performs a comprehensive analysis of your LinkedIn advertising campaigns, evaluating audience targeting precision (job titles, company sizes, industries, seniority), ad format effectiveness, Lead Gen Form completion rates, content engagement metrics, and LinkedIn-specific features like Matched Audiences, Document Ads, and Thought Leader Ads. It accounts for LinkedIn's higher CPMs by focusing on lead quality over volume.

Use Ads LinkedIn when your B2B pipeline depends on LinkedIn as a primary channel, when cost per lead is rising without corresponding improvements in lead quality, or when you are exploring LinkedIn's newer ad formats and need guidance on adoption. It is especially relevant for companies selling to enterprise buyers where precise firmographic targeting is critical.

Example: Run Ads LinkedIn to discover that your campaigns target "Marketing Manager" by job title but miss equivalent titles like "Growth Lead" and "Demand Gen Manager" (expanding your addressable audience by 40%), your Lead Gen Forms have a 35% completion rate that could increase to 50% by removing the company revenue field, and Thought Leader Ads from your CEO's profile are generating 3x the engagement of your brand page content ads at half the CPM.
