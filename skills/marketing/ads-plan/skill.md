Ads Plan creates a comprehensive paid advertising strategy from scratch, including platform selection, budget allocation, campaign architecture, audience segmentation, creative brief templates, measurement framework, and a phased rollout timeline. It uses industry-specific templates and benchmarks to set realistic targets and recommends a testing roadmap for the first 90 days.

Use Ads Plan when launching paid advertising for a new product or business, when restructuring an underperforming ad program, when entering a new market or geography, or when building an annual media plan. It provides the strategic foundation that individual platform skills then execute against.

Example: Tell Ads Plan you are a B2B SaaS company with a $30K monthly budget targeting mid-market companies in North America. It generates a plan allocating 45% to Google Search (brand + non-brand), 30% to LinkedIn (decision-maker targeting), 15% to Meta (retargeting + lookalikes), and 10% for experimentation. Each platform section includes recommended campaign structure, audience definitions, KPI targets based on industry benchmarks, and a creative production schedule.
