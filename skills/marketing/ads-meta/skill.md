Ads Meta provides a thorough analysis of your Meta (Facebook and Instagram) advertising ecosystem including campaign structure, Advantage+ configurations, audience overlap between ad sets, pixel and Conversions API health, creative performance by format and placement, frequency management, and attribution settings. It evaluates both prospecting and retargeting funnels to ensure efficient full-funnel coverage.

Use Ads Meta when Meta is a significant portion of your paid media mix, when iOS privacy changes have impacted your tracking and you need to verify your measurement setup, when scaling spend and encountering audience saturation, or when transitioning to Advantage+ campaign types.

Example: Run Ads Meta to find that your Conversions API is only matching 60% of events (below the 80% benchmark), three ad sets have 45% audience overlap causing you to bid against yourself, your Advantage+ Shopping campaign is spending 80% of budget on existing customers rather than prospecting, and your Instagram Reels placement is delivering 2.1x ROAS compared to 0.8x on Facebook News Feed but only receives 10% of impressions due to placement optimization settings.
