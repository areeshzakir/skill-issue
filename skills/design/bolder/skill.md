Bolder takes conservative, safe-looking designs and pushes them toward more visually striking and memorable territory. It identifies opportunities to amplify typography scale, introduce bolder color contrasts, add dramatic whitespace, use asymmetric layouts, and incorporate stronger visual hierarchy. The goal is to make the interface stand out without sacrificing usability.

Use Bolder when your design feels generic, corporate, or indistinguishable from competitors. It is ideal for landing pages, marketing sites, portfolio pages, and any interface where visual impact directly affects conversion or engagement. Bolder respects your existing brand palette but pushes it to its expressive limits.

Example: You have a SaaS landing page with standard centered sections, 16px body text, and muted gray accents. Run Bolder to transform it with oversized hero typography at 72px, full-bleed color sections, dramatic before/after comparisons, and bold geometric accent shapes that create a distinctive visual identity.
