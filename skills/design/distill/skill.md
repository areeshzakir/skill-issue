Distill analyzes your interface and systematically removes unnecessary complexity, redundant elements, and visual noise to reveal the essential experience underneath. It identifies elements that can be removed, combined, or hidden behind progressive disclosure without losing functionality. The result is a cleaner, more focused interface that reduces cognitive load and improves task completion rates.

Use Distill when your interface has grown cluttered over time through feature additions, when users report feeling overwhelmed, or when you want to simplify a complex workflow into fewer steps. It is particularly effective for settings pages, dashboards with too many widgets, and forms with excessive fields.

Example: Your user profile settings page has 40 fields visible at once across 6 sections. Run Distill to reorganize it into 3 primary sections with the 12 most-used fields visible by default, advanced options tucked behind expandable panels, and related fields grouped into logical clusters. The skill identifies 8 fields that can be auto-derived and 5 that are rarely changed and should be deprioritized.
