Clarify reviews and rewrites the text content within your interface to make it clearer, more helpful, and more human. It targets error messages, button labels, form placeholders, tooltips, empty states, confirmation dialogs, and onboarding instructions. Every rewrite prioritizes plain language, specific guidance, and a tone consistent with your brand voice.

Use Clarify when users report confusion about what to do next, when support tickets reference unclear messaging, or when your interface has accumulated developer-written placeholder copy that was never refined. It is particularly valuable for error states where users need actionable guidance rather than technical jargon.

Example: Your form displays "Error: Invalid input" when a user enters a malformed email. Run Clarify to transform this into "Please enter a valid email address (e.g., name@company.com)" with a specific visual indicator next to the field. It also rewrites your empty search results from "No results found" to "No results for 'widget'. Try a broader search term or browse categories."
