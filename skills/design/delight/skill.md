Delight identifies opportunities to inject moments of joy, surprise, and personality into your interface without compromising usability. It suggests playful micro-interactions, clever empty states, rewarding completion animations, easter eggs, personality-rich loading states, and celebratory moments that make your product memorable and emotionally resonant.

Use Delight when your interface is functional but feels sterile or forgettable. It is ideal for consumer-facing products, onboarding sequences, achievement milestones, and any interaction where positive emotion reinforces user engagement and retention. Delight is careful to suggest enhancements that feel authentic to your brand rather than gratuitous.

Example: Your task management app marks completed items with a simple strikethrough. Run Delight to add a satisfying confetti burst when all tasks in a list are completed, a playful illustration for empty inbox states ("You're all caught up!"), and a subtle bounce animation when dragging tasks between columns. Each suggestion includes implementation code and guidelines on when to restrain versus amplify the effect.
